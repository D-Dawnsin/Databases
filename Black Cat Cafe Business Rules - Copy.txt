1) Multiple cafés are located in the same city

2) Employees in each city are supervised by one city manager, and the city managers 
are supervised by the regional manager.  Each café has a unique ID number. 

3) Some customers use the Black Cat app to order their drinks before they arrive at the café.
 
4) Each drink order is supposed to be completed in 3 minutes or less, with hot drink 
temperature at 160 F and cold drink temperature at 40 F. 

5) If customers complain, they get a free drink coupon for $5, and the employee who 
prepared the drink gets a warning in his or her email, cc’d to the manager.
